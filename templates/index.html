Byte 1: Fusion data table

Research Question: Is there a relation between the top industries ranks and their physical location? To answer this question, I looked into data of the industry week magazine, that ranks the top 500 hundred industries in the US based on revenue. Then I color coded the industries by ranks in groups of 100s up to 300 (the last 200 are group together). The map shows that there is a certain tendency for the most profitable companies to be around major urban centers, and we can found them both on the east and west coast.
